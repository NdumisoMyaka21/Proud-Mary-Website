Welcome

Our Story

Welcome to Proud Mary, nestled in the heart of Rosebank's foodie district. A modern mid-century-inspired all-day eatery and wine bar to suit every taste.

Our home is in the New York-inspired building called "The Bank" where oxblood, teal, and emerald tones meet plush fabrics, natural wood, terrazzo, and brass to hark back to New York's golden age.

Our extensive menu celebrates cuisines from around the world, offering a refined, casual, and modern approach to dining. For wine lovers, we champion small-batch, local, and independent wineries at superb price points.
